The core of our business :

Future Energy Fabrica for Elektrik ( FEFA Power solutions ) is an electrical equipment design company, specializing in low voltage energy performance in terms of safety, service continuity, quality and energy efficiency.

Experience

Equipped with a team that has a total experience of more than 75 years in the field of power supply solutions, technical support, we can make a difference in the power market.

Location

Positioned in Turkey, the bridge between the west and east and the link between the north and the south, the heart of the silk trade road, gives our company the ability to provide the optimum solutions within optimum time frame and competitive logistic benefits to clients.

Clients

Our portfolio of clients covers a wide spectrum from using our products in personal applications to healthcare, data center, military and industrial fields, wherever power stability, quality and continuity is a concern we are there to assist.
